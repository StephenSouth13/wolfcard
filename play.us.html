Gameplay

Goal:

  • Werewolf team: Eliminate villagers until their number equals the wolves.
  • Villager team: Identify and eliminate hidden werewolves.

Players: 5 – 15

Game Master: In-game NPC

Game start: Players are randomly assigned roles (wolf, villager, seer, etc.)

Game Phases

Day: Discuss and vote. Eliminated players will reveal their roles.

Night: NPC calls special roles to perform their actions.

  • Basic role order: Werewolf → Seer → Guardian → Witch
  • With 10+ players: Cupid → Witch → Traitor → ...

Scoring & Rewards

Players earn points based on performance to rank up.

Role Distribution

5–10 players: 1–3 Wolves, the rest villagers and possibly special roles.

11+ players: Add special roles (Witch, Hunter, etc.) for balance.

Example with 15 players: 3 Wolves (1 normal, 1 sick, 1 gray), 8 Villagers (5 normal + 3 special), 2 third-party roles.

Characters & Abilities

Villager team: Village Chief, Normal Villager, Seer, Witch, Guardian, Cupid, Hunter,...

Werewolf team: Normal Wolf, Alpha Wolf, Sick Wolf, Baby Wolf, Traitor...

Third Party: Gray Wolf, Piper, Doomsayer, Mirror Image...
