Game Concept

Wolf Card is a strategic card game where each player secretly plays a unique role in a mysterious village. Every match is a mind game, where you must rely on logic and instinct to survive and protect your side.

  • Werewolves: eliminate others without getting caught.
  • Villagers: work together to identify and defeat the wolves.
  • Unique roles make each game fresh, unpredictable, and exciting.

About the Team

We are a group of passionate students from VTC Academy, driven by creativity, teamwork, and a love for games and technology. Wolf Card was developed as an academic project — but with a professional mindset and real-world inspiration.
